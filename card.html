Budgerigar

Budgerigars will chew on anything they can find to keep their beaks trimmed. Mineral blocks (ideally enriched with iodine), cuttlebone and soft wooden pieces are suitable for this activity.

  • Scientific Name : Melopsittacus undulatus
  • Average lifespan : 5-8 years in captivity
  • Weight : 40 grams
  • Origin : Australia

Budgerigars are naturally green and yellow with black, scalloped markings on the nape, back and wings, but have been bred in captivity with colouring in blues, whites, yellows, greys and even with small crests. Budgerigars are popular pets around the world due to their small size, low cost and ability to mimic human speech.
